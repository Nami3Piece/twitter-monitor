## 🚀 Daily X Digest 网站改版重大更新

亲爱的社区成员，

我们今日对 [monitor.dailyxdigest.uk](https://monitor.dailyxdigest.uk) 进行了一系列重大功能升级，旨在让你更快获取各生态最重要的信息。

---

### ✨ 新功能一览

#### 📌 官方动态区块（全新上线）
四个监测项目（ARKREEN、绿色比特币、TLAY、AI Renaissance）的项目页面，现在顶部均会显示官方账号的：
- **左侧**：官方账号的 **置顶帖**（官方精选内容）
- **右侧**：官方账号的 **最新帖子**
- 点击账号名可直接跳转至 X 官方主页

#### 📰 今日要闻置顶
每个项目页面的 AI 每日摘要要闻，现在直接显示在推文列表上方，进入项目页即可看到当日最重要的一条新闻。

#### 🔗 链接可点击
核心洞察和今日要闻中的 `[链接]` 现已渲染为可点击的蓝色超链接，直接跳转至原始 X 推文。

#### 🎙️ 历史语音回放
`/digest` 页面现已恢复浮动播放器（支持中英文切换、倍速播放），切换历史日期后音频自动加载，与文字内容始终同步。

#### 🏠 首页今日头条
首页的「新闻每日必看」4 张卡片，现在优先显示 AI 每日摘要 Top-1 新闻，而不再是单纯按互动量排序，确保内容与当日分析保持一致。

## 🚀 Major Update — Daily X Digest

Dear community members,

We've shipped several major upgrades to [monitor.dailyxdigest.uk](https://monitor.dailyxdigest.uk) today, making it easier to get the most important information from each ecosystem at a glance.

---

### ✨ What's New

#### 📌 Official Account Banner (New!)
All four project tabs (ARKREEN, GreenBTC, TLAY, AI Renaissance) now feature a dedicated official account section at the top:
- **Left card**: The official account's **pinned tweet** (their highlighted content)
- **Right card**: The official account's **latest tweet**
- Click the account name to jump directly to their X profile

#### 📰 Digest News Pinned to Project Pages
Today's AI-generated top news for each project now appears pinned above the tweet list when you enter any project tab — no more scrolling to find what matters most.

#### 🔗 Clickable Source Links
`[链接]` / `[link]` items in the Core Insight and Today's News sections are now rendered as clickable blue hyperlinks leading directly to the original X posts.

#### 🎙️ Historical Audio Playback Restored
The `/digest` page now has the full floating audio player (with zh/en toggle and playback speed control). Switching to a past date auto-loads that day's audio, always in sync with the text.

#### 🏠 Featured Cards From Digest
The four "Must-Read News" cards on the homepage now surface the AI Digest Top-1 item for each project, keeping homepage cards aligned with the daily analysis.
